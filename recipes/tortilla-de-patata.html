Tortilla de patata

Tortilla de patata, also known as Spanish omelette or tortilla espa√±ola, is a simple and tasty dish made with potatoes, onions, eggs, and olive oil

Ingredients

  • 2-3 medium-sized potatoes
  • 1/2 onion
  • 4-5 eggs
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1/2 cup olive oil

Steps to follow:

  1. Peel and thinly slice 2-3 medium-sized potatoes and 1/2 onion.
  2. Heat about 1/2 cup of olive oil in a non-stick pan over medium heat.
  3. Add the potatoes and onions to the pan and cook for about 20 minutes, stirring occasionally, until they're soft but not browned.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together 4-5 eggs and season with salt and pepper.
  5. Drain the potatoes and onions, then add them to the egg mixture and stir to combine.
  6. Pour the mixture back into the pan and cook over low-medium heat for 8-10 minutes, until the bottom is set but the top is still runny.
  7. Use a plate to flip the tortilla over, then slide it back into the pan to cook the other side for an additional 2-3 minutes.
  8. Slide the tortilla onto a plate and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing into wedges.
